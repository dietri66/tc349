History

1955 - The first mascot for MSU, a papier-mache Spartan head made by Donald Pais, Kenneth Roberts, and Don Bauer from Theta XI Freterity.

1984 - Sigma Phy Epsilon introduced the first "gruff" Sparty with the unshaven look.

1989 - Development of a new Sparty mascot began and design duties were given to Real Characters, Inc. The Alumni Association funded the $8,500 needed to design and construct the costume. On September 16, 1989 the new Sparty mascot made its debut in front of Spartan Stadium.

2004 - Sparty took 1st place in the UCDA Mascot competition in Orlando, Florida. Sparty perfomed a routine entitled "Sparty Loves the 80s."

2005 - Sparty won the Mascot championship for the second straight year. This routine was entitled "Sparty's Favorite Movies" highlighting scenes from Indiana Jones, The Lion King, Superman, Titanic, and Bring It On.

2007 - Sparty won his third National Mascot Championship in four years. Sparty's skit entitled "Sparty's Spectacular" that included music and dance spoofs of Kiss, Justin Timberlake, and many other popular music artists.
