Full Sort Report

My sorting process for Foldable involved a closed sort. I included all of the categories the site contained. I then asked participants to sort the content pieces into categories they thought made sense. I then performed the sort myself.

My sort differed a bit from the actual site architecture. The assembly for the Foldable is covered within the about page on the actual site. I thought it belonged in the creation section. The creation section does not help users currently to understand how to fold their foldable. I also included foldable delivery info in the creation section so those looking at buying a Foldable know about their options for delivery.

My results varied from my participants quite a lot. The participant thought the "Share Foldable" content should be included within the blog. The participant also had numerous discrepancies as to the content that should be included in the main page. The participant did not include much other content in the Foldable creator.
